Twists

your hair is 90% of your selfie - Anonymous

Background

Twists are a really cute and simple way of keeping your hair out of your face .

Equipment

  • 1 Rat-tail comb
  • 1 hairbrush
  • Hair gel(optional)
  • Toothbrush(optional)
  • elastic bands(optional)
  • Silk/Satin pillowcase or headscarf(for protection while you sleep)

Method

  1. Start off with clean dry hair.I highly recommend that you wash and deep condition your hair the night before you plan to braid your hair.
  2. Using a Rat-tail comb divide your hair into 2 equal sections
  3. Brush out any knots that may be in your hair
  4. Take 2 sections of hair from the top your head and twist them with each other
  5. Grab the surrounding hair and add to your main twist.Once you've reached the nape of your neck braid normally.
  6. Secure the ends of your hair with an elastic band if necessary.
  7. If you want you can slick down your babyhairs with a soft toothbrush and some styling gel.All you have to do is to apply gel to your edges and brush them in a swirling motion with a tooth brush.This creates a cute swirls of hair around your hairline instead of a bushy mess!If you don't have baby hairs then that's fine too
  8. Alternatively you can leave your hair as is or make a sidefringe.I laid down my edges but sometimes I like to have two fringes to frame my face.
  9. If you plan to sleep with the twists in your hair I reccomended that you sleep with a silk/satin pillowcase or headscarf while you sleep so as to prevent brakage from you tugging your hair in your sleep.
