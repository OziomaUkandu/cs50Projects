Makeup

Background

this is quite a simple look to replicate and doesn't take much time at all! .

Equipment

  • Foundation
  • 1 foundation brush or beauty blender
  • Concealer
  • eyeshadow palette
  • eyeshadow brushes
  • eyebrow wax
  • angled brush
  • eyeliner
  • mascara
  • liquid lipstick
  • primer
  • Setting spray
  • highlighter or blush(depending on your preference)

Method

  1. Start off with a freshly washed and moisturised face.I highly recommend that you follow up with a primer so that you can make your look last longer.
  2. Dip your angled brush into your eyebrow wax and shape your eyebrows to your desired look.I like to make mine arched so you can do that if you want or opt for a straight brow
  3. Conceal the edges of your eyebrow using concealer.This will make the shape more defined and crisp.
  4. Conceal your undereyes in a triangle shape and highlight your forehead with concealer it with your natural hair.Using a foundation brush or beauty blender, blend the concealer into your skin.
  5. Using your eyeshadow brushes and and eyeshadow palette create a smokey eye using the colours of your choice
  6. Apply lipstick of your choice.I used 2 different shades of purple for my lips.I applied the lighter colour to the centre of my lips and used the darker colour to the corners of my lips. Then I blended the 2 colours to create a shadow or ombr√© effect.If you want extra preciscion you can also conceal around your lips.
  7. Now apply your foundation using your beauty blender or foundation brush.
  8. Highlight your cheekbones using your favourite highlighter or apply blush.Choose one or the other not both!Of course if you don't fancy either blush or highlight then don't .
  9. Lightly mist your face with a setting spray to lock in all the pigments.Don't go overboard with the spray as this can result in your face looking sweaty and wet
